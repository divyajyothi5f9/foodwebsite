üçõ Chicken Biryani Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups Basmati Rice
  • 500g Chicken
  • 2 Onions (sliced)
  • 1 cup Yogurt
  • 2 tbsp Biryani Masala
  • Salt & Oil

Steps:

  1. Wash and soak rice for 30 minutes.
  2. Fry onions until golden brown.
  3. Add chicken, yogurt and spices. Cook well.
  4. Cook rice separately until 80% done.
  5. Layer rice and chicken in a pot.
  6. Cook on low flame for 15 minutes.
  7. Serve hot.
